2 Corinthians 4

1. 2Cor 4:1,2 - Paul said that since it is by God's mercy that they are engaged in this ministry, they do not lose heart. Rather, what had they renounced?

  1. reckless
  2. boastful and blameless
  3. shameful things that one hides
  4. hopeless
2. 2Cor 4:2 - Paul said that they refuse to practice cunning or to falsify God's word. On the contrary, by the open statement of the truth they commend themselves to what in the sight of God?

  1. public opinion
  2. every man's guilt
  3. praise
  4. the conscience of everyone
3. 2Cor 4:3,4 - Fill in the blanks: “And even if our ______ is veiled, it is veiled to those who are perishing. In their case the god of this world has blinded the minds of the unbelievers, so that they cannot see the light of the ______ of the glory of Christ, who is the image of God.”

  1. gospel
  2. grace
  3. giving
  4. generosity
4. 2Cor 4:6 - Who said: "Let light shine out of darkness"?

  1. John the Baptist
  2. God
  3. Paul
  4. Adam
5. 2Cor 4:7 - Paul said that they have this treasure in jars of what, to show that this extraordinary power belongs to God and does not come from them?

  1. copper
  2. men's hearts
  3. clay
  4. grape jelly
6. 2Cor 4:8,9 - Which of the following pairs did Paul NOT use to describe his ministry?

  1. afflicted in every way, but not crushed;
  2. perplexed, but not driven to despair;
  3. persecuted, but not abolished;
  4. struck down, but not destroyed.
7. 2Cor 4:11-13 - Paul said that those who live are always being given up to death for Jesus' sake, so that his life may be made visible in their mortal flesh. He said that it is written: "I believed, and so I" what?

  1. obeyed
  2. repented
  3. spoke
  4. received
8. 2Cor 4:14 - Paul said that the one who raised the Lord Jesus would also raise them with Jesus, and would bring them with the Corinthians where?

  1. in the temple
  2. into his presence
  3. at Corinth
  4. in their homes
9. 2Cor 4:16 - Paul said that they do not lose heart. Even though their outer nature is wasting away, yet day by day their inner nature is being what?

  1. rescued
  2. renewed
  3. reviewed
  4. rude
10. 2Cor 4:17 - Paul said that this slight momentary affliction was preparing them for an eternal what beyond all measure?

  1. weight of glory
  2. fame and fortune
  3. moment of glory
  4. respect
11. 2Cor 4:18 - Paul said that they look not at what can be seen, but at what cannot be seen. For what can be seen is temporary, but what cannot be seen is what?

  1. eternal
  2. secret
  3. hidden
  4. transparent

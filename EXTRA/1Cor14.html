  2. prophesy
  3. speak in tongues

  1. teachings
  2. prophecies
  3. promises
  4. mysteries

  1. learning
  2. growth
  3. consolation
  4. encouragement

  1. the church
  2. the lost
  3. the whole world
  4. his family
5. 1Cor 14:5 - Paul said that he would like all of them to speak in tongues, but even more to prophesy. One who prophesies is greater than one who speaks in tongues, unless someone does what, so that the church may be built up?

  1. shouts
  2. preaches
  3. prays
  4. interprets

  1. in tongues
  2. softly
  3. loudly
  4. in love
7. 1Cor 14:9 - Paul said that unless in a tongue they uttered speech that was intelligible, how would anyone know what was being said? For they would be speaking into what?

  1. the air
  2. deaf ears
  3. a noisy clatter
  4. emptiness
8. 1Cor 14:10-12 - Paul said that all sounds have meaning, but if he didn't know the meaning of a sound, he would be a foreigner to the speaker. He said that since they were eager for spiritual gifts, they should strive to excel in them for what?

  1. attracting attention
  2. building up the church
  3. multiplying
  4. overflowing

  1. made up
  2. empty
  3. unproductive
  4. clouded

  1. sermon
  2. prayer
  3. petition
  4. thanksgiving
11. 1Cor 14:18,19 - Paul thanked God that he spoke in tongues more than all of them; nevertheless, in church he would rather speak five words with his mind, in order to instruct others, than how many words in a tongue?

  1. 50
  2. 500
  3. 5,000
  4. 10,000
12. 1Cor 14:21 - Fill in the blank: "By people of strange tongues and by the lips of foreigners I will speak to this people; yet even then they will not ______ to me, says the Lord."

  1. call
  2. pray
  3. sing
  4. listen
13. 1Cor 14:22 - Paul said tongues were a sign not for believers but for unbelievers. What did he say was not for unbelievers but for believers?

  1. faith
  2. teaching
  3. prophecy
  4. prayer
14. 1Cor 14:23 - Paul said if the whole church came together and all spoke in tongues, and outsiders or unbelievers came in, would they not say that they were out of their what?

  1. league
  2. mind
  3. territory
  4. comfort zone
15. 1Cor 14:24,25 - Paul said if all prophesy, an unbeliever or outsider who enters is reproved by all and called to account by all. After the secrets of the heart are disclosed, that person will bow down and worship God, declaring what?

  1. "Praise God!"
  2. "Hallelujah!"
  3. "God is really among you!"
  4. "You rock!"
16. 1Cor 14:26 - Paul said when they came together, each one had a hymn, a lesson, a revelation, a tongue, or an interpretation. Let all things be done for what?

  1. building up
  2. the community
  3. the lost
  4. the vocal chords
17. 1Cor 14:27,28 - Paul said if anyone spoke in a tongue, there should be only two or at most three, and each in turn; and let one interpret. But if there was no one to interpret, the speaker should keep silent in church and speak to himself and God. What was the highest number of people that he said should speak in tongues?

  1. one
  2. two
  3. three
  4. four

  2. keep speaking
  3. slow down
  4. be silent

  2. order
  3. comfort
  4. grace

  1. in control
  2. subordinate
  3. in training
  4. in love

  1. outside
  2. at home
  3. in the courtyard
  4. in the foyer

  1. condemned
  2. forgiven
  3. honored
  4. not to be recognized

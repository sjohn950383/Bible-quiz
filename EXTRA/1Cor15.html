1 Corinthians 15

  1. falsely
  2. in faith
  3. blindly
  4. in vain

  1. tradition
  2. legend
  3. the scriptures
  4. the 3 wise men

  1. 5
  2. 50
  3. 500
  4. more than five hundred

  1. untimely
  2. breach
  3. caesarean
  4. dead

  1. prosecuted
  2. persecuted
  3. preached
  4. poached
6. 1Cor 15:10 - Fill in the blanks: "But by the _____ of God I am what I am, and his _____ toward me has not been in vain. On the contrary, I worked harder than any of them--yet not I, but the _____ of God that is with me."

  1. love
  2. teaching
  3. grace
  4. hope

  2. invisible

  4. strengthened

  4. pitied

  4. in y'all

  4. Ephesus

  4. die

  4. countries

  2. grows
  3. produces fruit
  4. dies
20. 1Cor 15:37-39 - Paul said that when you sow, you do not sow the body that is to be, but a bare seed, but God gives it a body as he has chosen and to each kind of seed he gives its own body. He said that human beings, animals, birds, and fish all have something that is not alike. What word was he referring to?

  1. feet
  2. faces
  3. flesh
  4. feathers
21. 1Cor 15:40,41 - Paul said that there are heavenly bodies and there are earthly bodies. He said that the heavenly and earthly bodies as well as the sun, moon, and stars each has something that differs from the other in glory. What word was he referring to?

  1. surfaces
  2. glory
  3. size
  4. shape
22. 1Cor 15:42-44 - Concerning the resurrection of the dead, the body that is sown is different than the body that is raised. Which of these pairs of descriptions was NOT used by Paul to describe "sown vs. raised"?

  1. perishable/imperishable
  2. dishonor/glory
  3. weakness/power
  4. physical body/physical mind

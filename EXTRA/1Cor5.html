1 Corinthians 5

1. 1Cor 5:1 - It is actually reported that there is sexual immorality among the Corinthians, and of a kind that is not found even among whom?

  1. sinners
  2. demons
  3. pagans
  4. the Gentiles
2. 1Cor 5:2 - Paul said that they are arrogant and should have mourned, so that he who has done this would have been removed from among what?

  1. the city
  2. their way
  3. the family
  4. you (them)
3. 1Cor 5:3 - Paul said that though absent in body, he had already pronounced judgment as if he were present. How is he present with them?

  1. in spirit
  2. in mind
  3. figuratively
  4. in their dreams
4. 1Cor 5:5 - Paul said they are to hand this man over to whom for the destruction of the flesh, so that his spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord?

  1. the magistrates
  2. Satan
  3. the Sanhedrin
  4. the mob
5. 1Cor 5:6 - Paul asked, "Do you not know that a little yeast leavens the whole batch of dough?" What did Paul say was not a good thing?

  1. their boasting
  2. their stealing
  3. their cheating
  4. their baking
6. 1Cor 5:7 - Paul told them to clean out the old yeast so that they may be a new batch, as they really are unleavened. For Christ had been sacrificed as our what?

  1. paschal lamb
  2. scapegoat
  3. fatted calf
  4. dove
7. 1Cor 5:8 - Fill in the blanks: "Therefore, let us celebrate the festival, not with the old yeast, the yeast of malice and evil, but with the unleavened bread of _________ and _____."

  1. truth, light
  2. faith, love
  3. joy, peace
  4. sincerity, truth
8. 1Cor 5:9,10 - Paul wrote in his letter not to associate with sexually immoral persons, not at all meaning the immoral of this world, or the greedy and robbers, or idolaters. In that case, he said they would need to do what?

  1. abandon the faith
  2. leave the church
  3. abandon their families
  4. go out of the world
9. 1Cor 5:11 - Paul said they must not associate with anyone who bears the name of brother or sister who is sexually immoral or greedy, or is an idolater, reviler, drunkard, or robber. With such a one, they should not even do what?

  1. talk
  2. associate
  3. eat
  4. drink
10. 1Cor 5:12,13 - Paul asked what he had to do with judging those outside, since they are to judge those who are inside. Who did he say will judge those outside?

  1. the high priest
  2. the Spirit
  3. God
  4. the elders

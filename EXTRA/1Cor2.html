1 Corinthians 2

1. 1Cor 2:1 - When Paul came to the brothers and sisters, he did not come in lofty words or wisdom proclaiming what to them?

  1. the love of Christ
  2. the fruit of the Spirit
  3. the message of hope
  4. the mystery of God
2. 1Cor 2:2 - Paul decided to know nothing among the Corinthians except Jesus Christ, and him what?

  1. baptized
  2. glorified
  3. crucified
  4. resurrected
3. 1Cor 2:3 - Paul came to the Corinthians in weakness and in fear and in much what?

  1. weeping
  2. trembling
  3. anxiety
  4. perspiration
4. 1Cor 2:4,5 - Paul's speech and proclamation were not with plausible words of wisdom, but with a demonstration of the Spirit and of power, so that their faith might rest not on human wisdom but on what?

  1. the power of God
  2. the cross of Christ
  3. his shoulders
  4. human knowledge
5. 1Cor 2:6 - Paul said that among the mature they do speak wisdom, though it is not a wisdom of this age or of the rulers of this age, who are doomed to what?

  1. nothing
  2. perish
  3. understand
  4. dinner
6. 1Cor 2:7,8 - Paul said that they speak God's wisdom, secret and hidden, which God decreed before the ages for our glory. None of the rulers of this age understood this; for if they had, they would not have done what?

  1. left their first love
  2. crucified the Lord of glory
  3. criticized the disciples
  4. ruled with an iron fist
7. 1Cor 2:9 - Fill in the blank: "What no eye has seen, nor ear heard, nor the human heart conceived, what God has ________ for those who love him"

  1. done
  2. created
  3. prepared
  4. forsaken
8. 1Cor 2:10 - Who searches everything, even the depths of God?

  1. Satan
  2. true believers
  3. Gabriel
  4. the Spirit
9. 1Cor 2:11 - What human being knows what is truly human except the human spirit that is within? So also no one comprehends what is truly God's except who?

  1. the archangel
  2. the Spirit of God
  3. the spirit of truth
  4. preachers
10. 1Cor 2:12 - We have received not the spirit of the world, but the Spirit that is from God, so that we may understand the what bestowed on us by God?

  1. gifts
  2. miracles
  3. powers
  4. wisdom
11. 1Cor 2:13 - Paul said that they speak of these things in words not taught by human wisdom but taught by the Spirit, interpreting spiritual things to who?

  1. the rulers of this age
  2. holy people
  3. those who are spiritual
  4. the mature
12. 1Cor 2:14 - Those who are unspiritual do not receive the gifts of God's Spirit, for they are foolishness to them, and they are unable to understand them because they are spiritually what?

  1. expressed
  2. remembered
  3. requested
  4. discerned
13. 1Cor 2:15 - Those who are spiritual discern all things, and they are themselves subject to no one else's what?

  1. requirements
  2. scrutiny
  3. acceptance
  4. religion
14. 1Cor 2:16 - Fill in the blank: "For who has known the mind of the Lord so as to ________ him?"

  1. serve
  2. command
  3. instruct
  4. reject

1 Corinthians 1

  1. Crispus
  2. Gaius
  3. Cephas
  4. Sosthenes

  1. fearless
  2. saints
  3. full of wisdom
  4. leaders

  1. praying
  2. love
  3. knowledge
  4. ways

  1. food
  2. shelter
  3. testimony
  4. spiritual gift
5. 1Cor 1:8,9 - Paul said that Christ would strengthen them to the end, so they may be blameless on the day of our Lord Jesus Christ. He also said that God, who had called them into the fellowship of his Son, is what?

  1. loving
  2. forgiving
  3. wonderful
  4. faithful
6. 1Cor 1:10 - Paul appeals to them that they be in agreement and that there be no divisions among them, but that they be united in the same purpose and the same what?

  1. mind
  2. body
  3. love
  4. joy
7. 1Cor 1:11 - Whose people had reported to Paul that there were quarrels among the Corinthians?

  1. Amanda's
  2. Brittney's
  3. Chloe's
  4. Dauntae's

  1. Paul
  2. Apollos
  3. Cephas
  4. Sosthenes
9. 1Cor 1:13,14 - Paul asked, "Has Christ been divided? Was Paul crucified for you? Or were you baptized in the name of Paul?" He thanked God he baptized none of them except for which two individuals?

  1. Crispus and Gaius
  2. Titus and Timothy
  3. Cephas and Silas
  4. Lilo and Stitch

  1. Chloe
  2. Sosthenes
  3. Stephanas
  4. Cephas
11. 1Cor 1:17 - Christ did not send Paul to baptize but to proclaim the gospel, and not with eloquent wisdom, so that the cross of Christ might not be emptied of its what?

  1. blood
  2. power
  3. pain
  4. glory
12. 1Cor 1:18 - The message about the cross is foolishness to those who are perishing, but to us who are being saved it is what?

  1. the hope of tomorrow
  2. the power of God
  3. the emblem of suffering
  4. wisdom
13. 1Cor 1:19 - Fill in the blank: "I will destroy the wisdom of the wise, and the discernment of the discerning I will _________."

  1. increase
  2. decrease
  3. encourage
  4. thwart
14. 1Cor 1:20,21 - Paul asked, "Where is the one who is wise? Where is the scribe? Where is the debater of this age? Has not God made foolish the wisdom of the world?" He said God decided, through the foolishness of our proclamation, to save who?

  1. those who believe
  2. the Jews
  3. the Gentiles
  4. those who deserve
15. 1Cor 1:22 - Who did Paul say demand signs?

  1. Greeks
  2. priests
  3. Jews
  4. Pharisees
16. 1Cor 1:22 - Who did Paul say desire wisdom?

  1. Greeks
  2. priests
  3. Jews
  4. Pharisees
17. 1Cor 1:23 - Paul said that proclaiming Christ crucified was what to Jews?

  1. hope
  2. blaspheme
  3. a mystery
  4. a stumbling block
18. 1Cor 1:23 - Paul said that proclaiming Christ crucified was what to Gentiles?

  1. foolishness
  2. forgiveness
  3. wisdom
  4. confusing
19. 1Cor 1:24,25 - Paul said that to those who are the called, Christ is the power of God and the wisdom of God. For God's foolishness is wiser than human wisdom, and what is stronger than human strength?

  1. the strength of God
  2. God's weakness
  3. the wind
  4. prayer

  1. the weak
  2. the timid
  3. the strong
  4. the shameless
21. 1Cor 1:28,29 - Paul said that God chose what is low and despised in the world, things that are not, to reduce to nothing things that are, so that no one might do what in the presence of God?

  1. complain
  2. stand
  3. bow
  4. boast
22. 1Cor 1:30 - Paul said, "He is the source of your life in Christ Jesus, who became for us wisdom from God, and righteousness and sanctification and what?"

  1. wisdom
  2. redemption
  3. perfection
  4. peace
23. 1Cor 1:31 - Fill in the blank: "Let the one who boasts, boast in the ____."

  1. Spirit
  2. heavens
  3. creation
  4. Lord

2 Corinthians 5

  1. temple
  2. tent
  3. shell
  4. house

  1. life
  2. clouds
  3. love
  4. a whale
3. 2Cor 5:5 - He who has prepared us for this very thing is God, who has given us the Spirit as a what?

  1. sign
  2. gift
  3. withdrawal
  4. guarantee

  1. might
  2. right
  3. sight
  4. light

  1. the judgment seat of Christ
  2. our accusers
  3. the saints in glory
  4. Judge Judy

  1. run away
  2. gain confidence
  3. remain calm
  4. persuade others
7. 2Cor 5:12 - Fill in the blank: “We are not commending ourselves to you again, but are giving you an opportunity to boast about us, so that you may be able to answer those who boast in outward appearance rather than in what is in the _____.”

  1. unseen
  2. hidden places
  3. dark
  4. heart
8. 2Cor 5:14 - Paul said that Christ's love urges them on, because they were convinced that one has died for all, and therefore all did what?

  2. died
  3. lied
  4. fried
9. 2Cor 5:16,17 - Paul said, 'From now on, therefore, we regard no one from a human point of view. Even though we once knew Christ from a human point of view, we know him no longer in that way.' So if anyone is in Christ, there is a new what?

  1. creation
  2. friend
  3. relative
  4. angel
10. 2Cor 5:18-20 - God has reconciled us to himself through Christ and given us the ministry of reconciliation: that God was reconciling the world to himself in Christ, not counting their trespasses against them. And he has entrusted to us the message of reconciliation. We are therefore what?

  1. reconcilers
  2. children
  3. ambassadors for Christ
  4. congregation
11. 2Cor 5:21 - For our sake God made him to be sin who knew no sin, so that in him we might become the what?

  1. redemption of man
  2. reality of humanity
  3. righteousness of God
  4. completeness of life

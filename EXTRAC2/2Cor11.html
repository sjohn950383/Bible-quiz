10. 2Cor 11:18-21 - Fill in the blanks: "Since many ______ according to human standards, I will also ______. You gladly put up with fools, being wise yourselves! To my shame, I must say, we were too weak for that! But whatever anyone dares to ______ of—I am speaking as a fool—I also dare to ______ of."

  2. think
  3. laugh
  4. complain
